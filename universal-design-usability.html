Universal Design and Usability

Understanding how your clients or users think is the initial step to a great design. Accessibility and usability have been crucial concerns for instructional design in online educational systems. With the pervasiveness of online education systems and course management systems (CMS), the conveyance of available and easy to understand user interface end up pivotal to a fruitful online experience. Here is a redesign of PeopleSoft application which is extensively used in different universities by students to search for courses and enroll. This project significantly emphasize on low physical effort, equitable use, flexibility in use, size and space for approach and use, intuitiveness, flexible design, keybord accessibility, tollerance for error, perceptable information and user control.
