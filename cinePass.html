About CinePass

By learning about the signs and symptoms of smartphone addiction and how to break free of the habit, you can better balance your life. To deal with the issue, we suggest an idea that helps you beat your smartphone addiction. The idea is to keep track of your phone usage and help you reduce unnecessary use of it. We intend to call this application “SmartCurb”, a mobile application to curb your smartphone addiction.
